HR ANALYTICS REPORT

The objective of this HR Analytics Report project is to create a dashboard that helps visualize important information about the company's employees. It's like making a big, colorful picture that answers these key questions: How many employees has the company had since it started? How many employees are currently working at the company? How many employees have left (terminated)? What percentage of employees have left (termination rate)? How much total salary has been paid out to all employees? How many current employees are male or female? What is the education level of the employees? How happy are employees with their jobs? How balanced is the employees' work-life (work-life balance rate)? How many employees have been hired each quarter? How many employees have left each quarter? What are the trends for employee terminations every month? What are the trends for salaries every month? How is salary distributed based on how many years full-time employees or contractors have worked at the company? How is salary spread by the total working years of employees, broken down by gender? In simple terms, the project aims to build a dashboard that answers these questions, so the company can understand their workforce better and make good decisions about managing their employees.
